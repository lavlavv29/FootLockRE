Our Events

Where can you find us?

Explore our booths at upcoming events and discover the world of craftsmanship through our exquisite handmade leather men's shoes.

  • 24
    June

    Jakarta Sneakers Day (JSD)

    24-27 June 2023 || 10AM-20PM
    Hall 1, Indonesia Convention Exhibition (ICE) BSD, Tangerang

    Jakarta Sneakers Day is one of the largest sneakers and streetwear exhibitions in Indonesia. JSD has been present and continues to bring and share things about sneaker culture to a wider audience through events, both offline and online, and of course also through promotions through JSD's social media. JSD always believes in the spirit and vision of “Sneaker For All” where everything related to sneaker culture: be it knowledge, information, community, inside, and of course the shoes themselves, should (and even should) be accessible to everyone.


  • 13
    July

    Kreative Independent Creative Kommunity (KICK)

    13-15 July 2023 ||9AM-22PM
    Lapangan Pussenif, Bandung

    The Kreative Independent Creative which consists of entrepreneurs in Bandung, began anuual events since 2006. The Kick Festival, which was later shortened to KickFest, has become a movement that seeks to make local brands the host in their own country. KickFest visitors can buy various kinds of fashion products such as t-shirts, shirts, pants to accessories ranging from bags to shoes.


  • 11
    August

    Pasar Nusantara

    11-21 August 2023 ||11AM-22PM
    Ground Floor, Pondok Indah Mall 1, South Jakarta

    In order to celebrate the 77th anniversary of the Independence of the Republic of Indonesia, PIM is again presenting the Nusantara Market which will take place from 11 - 21 August 2023. One of the main events is "Galeri Sepatu dan Kerajinan Nusantara" which will be filled with various kinds of authentic creations by the hands of the nation's children. Visit our booth in 'Pasar Nusantara'!
